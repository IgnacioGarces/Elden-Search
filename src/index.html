Description

Usher of Death, Rosus, who shows the path to the catacombs throughout the Land Between, is depicted on this ritual axe. The dead easily lose their way, and have always been in sore need of a guiding hand. Unique Skill : Rosus's Summons Raise the axe aloft to summon those lost in death. Three skeletons will appear at a distance and attack in tandem before vanishing.
